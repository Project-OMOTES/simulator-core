Air-to-water heat pump asset
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

The **Air-to-Water Heat Pump Asset** is the entity-level representation of a
heat pump that uses ambient air as source and delivers heat to the thermal
network.

It captures the configuration needed to describe this specialized producer in
the simulation input model.

Implemented by
--------------

``omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.air_to_water_heat_pump``.
