Air-to-water heat pump asset
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
The **Air-to-Water Heat Pump Asset** represents a heat pump that uses ambient
air as source and delivers heat to the thermal network.

This asset is stored within the ``AirToWaterHeatPump`` class in the
``omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.air_to_water_heat_pump`` module.
