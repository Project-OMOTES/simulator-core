Ideal heat storage asset
++++++++++++++++++++++++
The **Ideal Heat Storage Asset** represents thermal storage behavior with
charge and discharge operation.

This asset is stored within the ``IdealHeatStorage`` class in the
``omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.ideal_heat_storage`` module.
