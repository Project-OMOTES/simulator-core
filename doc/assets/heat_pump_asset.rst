Heat pump asset
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

The **Heat Pump Asset** is the entity-level representation of a controllable
heat pump that delivers heat to the network.

It captures the configuration and ports needed to describe the asset in the
simulation input layer.

Implemented by
--------------

This reference page documents the ``HeatPump`` entity in
``omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.heat_pump``.

---------------------
