SolverPipe (Solver-Side Pipe Model)
===================================

The ``SolverPipe`` class is the solver-side pipe representation used to build
hydraulic and thermal equations for pipe segments.

Role
----

- Adds pipe-specific pressure-loss behavior.
- Adds thermal exchange and heat-loss behavior.
- Contributes equations for mass flow, pressure drop, and internal energy transport.
