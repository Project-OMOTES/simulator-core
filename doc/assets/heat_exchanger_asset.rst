Heat exchanger asset
++++++++++++++++++++
The **Heat Exchanger Asset** represents an asset that transfers thermal energy
between two hydraulic sides.

This asset is stored within the ``HeatExchanger`` class in the
``omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.heat_exchanger`` module.
