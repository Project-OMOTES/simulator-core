.. _junction-asset:

Junction asset
++++++++++++++

The **Junction Asset** is the entity-level representation of a network junction
used for connectivity and flow distribution between assets.

It defines a connection point where network branches meet and where mass and
energy balances are enforced by the solver.

Implemented by
--------------

This reference page documents the ``Junction`` entity in
``omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.junction``.
