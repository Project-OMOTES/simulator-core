Ates asset
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
The **ATES Asset** represents an **Aquifer Thermal Energy Storage (ATES)** system within the simulator. ATES enables the storage and retrieval of thermal energy from aquifers.

This asset is stored within the `AtesCluster` class in the `omotes_simulator_core.entities.assets.ates_cluster` module.
